Sustainability Through the Ages

Explore Intel's journey through time, discovering how innovation has shaped a more sustainable future for technology and our planet.

1968

Intel Founded

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore rename NM Electronics to Intel Corporation.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel debuts the 4004, the world's first commercial microprocessor.

1978

8086 Processor

Launch of the 8086 processor, establishing the x86 architecture.

1985

386 Processor

Introduction of the 386 processor with 32-bit architecture.

2006

Peak GHG Emissions

Intel reaches peak emissions and begins major sustainability investments.

2020

RISE Strategy

Launch of the RISE strategy and 2030 sustainability goals.

2022

Net-Zero by 2040

Commitment to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2040.

2023

99% Renewable Electricity

Intel achieves 99% renewable electricity usage globally.

2024

Sustainability Summit

Intel hosts its first Sustainability Summit for industry collaboration.
